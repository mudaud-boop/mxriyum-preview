Sticky date cake with
brown butter toffee.

M Mariyum · October 14, 2026 · 4.9 ★ (142 ratings)
A Sunday kind of cake. Serve warm.
Prep
15 min
Cook
40 min
Serves
8
Difficulty
Easy

This started as a craving for a date cake on a rainy Tuesday, and turned into one of the most saved recipes on the site. Medjool dates, brown butter, a toffee sauce you pour over while the cake is still warm.

I'd eat it for breakfast with coffee, for dessert with a scoop of vanilla, and straight from the pan at midnight — no judgement.

Ingredients

For the cake

  • 200g medjool dates, pitted
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 200ml boiling water
  • 115g salted butter, softened
  • 150g light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 180g self-rising flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

For the toffee sauce

  • 115g salted butter
  • 200g dark brown sugar
  • 200ml double cream
  • flaky sea salt, to finish

Method

01

Heat the oven to 180°C (350°F). Butter an 8-inch round cake pan and line the base with parchment. Pit the dates, then pour the boiling water over them with the baking soda. Let sit 10 minutes — they'll get soft and glossy.

the baking soda softens the dates and mellows any sharp edge — don't skip it.
02

Brown the 115g of butter for the sauce in a small pan — medium heat, swirling, until the milk solids are deep amber and it smells like toasted caramel. Off the heat, add the brown sugar and cream, whisk until smooth. Set aside.

03

In a stand mixer, cream the softened butter and sugar for the cake until pale. Add eggs one at a time, then vanilla. Fold in the flour, then the date mixture (with its liquid). It'll look loose — that's right.

04

Pour into the pan. Bake 38–42 minutes, until a skewer comes out with a few moist crumbs. While it's still warm, poke holes all over the top with a skewer and pour half the toffee sauce over — let it drink it in.

the warm cake + warm sauce step is the whole thing. trust.
05

Slice thick. Serve with the rest of the toffee sauce warmed, a scoop of vanilla ice cream, and a pinch of flaky salt. Leftovers keep 3 days, covered — rewarm gently.

Cook's notes.

Make ahead: Bake the cake a day ahead and make the toffee sauce fresh. Rewarm the cake in a low oven for 10 minutes before pouring sauce.

Dairy-free: Sub vegan butter and full-fat coconut cream. The toffee still browns beautifully.

Don't skip: Flaky salt on top. It's the whole point.
