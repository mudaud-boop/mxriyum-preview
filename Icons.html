Icons.
Twenty icons, all drawn on a 24×24 grid with 1.5px strokes, rounded caps & joins. Warm and precise — thin enough to sit next to the light serif, confident enough to read at 16px. Click any icon to copy its name as a token.

Principles.

The rules every icon follows — so they feel like a family, not a collection.
24 × 24 grid.
Every icon is drawn inside a 24-unit square with a 1.5-unit safe-area padding. Scale by multiplying.
1.5px stroke.
Thin enough to pair with Cormorant, heavy enough to hold at 16px. One weight across the system.
Round caps.
Stroke-linecap and stroke-linejoin are both round. Softens the geometry; feels kitchen-warm, not technical.
Stroke only.
No fills. Color via currentColor so icons inherit from surrounding text — including hover and state changes.
