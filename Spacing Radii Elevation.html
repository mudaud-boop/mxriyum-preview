Scaffolding.
Spacing, radii, elevation. The invisible rules. A 4-based spacing scale, a graduated radius set, and five elevation levels. Every component built from here on references these tokens — never magic numbers.

Principles.

Three rules every component follows. Break them on purpose, not by accident.
01
Spacing is rhythm.
All gaps, padding, and offsets come from a 4-based scale. Two sibling elements at --sp-5 + --sp-7 feel calm together; 13px + 19px never does.
02
Radii are soft.
Nothing is truly sharp. Even "none" is a choice — but most surfaces use --r-md or --r-lg. Pills for tags, circles for avatars.
03
Shadows are warm.
Shadows use rgba(46, 38, 33, …) — the ink color, not black. Subtly warm even at low opacity. Five levels, not a gradient.
